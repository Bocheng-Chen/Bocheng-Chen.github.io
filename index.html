My research focuses on computer security, with a specific aim to identify and mitigate realistic security vulnerabilities in LLM-based chatbot systems. I investigate the worst-case behaviors of chatbots in real-world settings and design secure systems, acknowledging that the probabilistic nature of LLMs makes it difficult to reliably enforce security policies.

Research Interests

  • Chatbot and Large Language Model Security
  • Adversarial Machine Learning (Jailbreaks, Backdoors)
  • Moral Reasoning and AI Alignment
  • Trustworthy AI Systems
