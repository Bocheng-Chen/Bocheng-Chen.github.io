Bocheng Chen

I'm a tenure-track Assistant Professor of Computer and Information Science at the University of Mississippi.

I study computer security, with a focus on risks in chatbot systems. My research aims to identify and mitigate realistic security vulnerabilities in LLM-based chatbot systems to ensure their secure operation.

My work explores questions such as: What is the worst-case behavior when chatbots are widely deployed in real-world settings, and can we design secure chatbot systems given that current large language models cannot reliably enforce security policies?

I collaborate closely with Guangliang Liu (Michigan State University) and Dr. Zoe Xi Chen (Nanyang Technological University) on Pragmatics and Computational Linguistics.
