I study computer security with a focus on risks in chatbot systems. My research aims to identify and mitigate realistic security vulnerabilities in LLM-based chatbot systems to ensure their secure operation.

I am also interested in the question: What is the worst-case behavior when chatbots are widely deployed in real-world settings, and can we design secure chatbot systems given that current large language models, due to their probabilistic nature, cannot reliably enforce security policies?

I collaborate closely with Guangliang Liu at Michigan State University and Dr Zoe Xi Chen at Nanyang Technological University on Pragmatics and Computational Linguistics.
